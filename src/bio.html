B I O

Iona Brown (b.1998)

is an interdisciplinary artist with a focus on sculpture.
She researches and visually interprets her findings through
selective mediums and techniques, specifically those of
contrasting beauty and brutality. Her installations portray
spaces of uncertainty and vulnerability, which may feel
uncomfortable to witness.
